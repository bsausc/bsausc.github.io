Inter Student Friendship Soccer League(ISFSL) 2011

BSA organized an international student friendship soccer tournament from October 1, 2011. Five countries participated in the tournament; Bangladesh, China, Egypt, Iran, and Turkey. All the matches werel be held in Storm Thurmond outdoor field#3. The final was held between Iran and Turkey on 12th November, 2011, and Turkey won the championship by beating Iran 2 goals to 1.  Lutfi from Turkey team was the highest scorer of the tournament with 10 goals.
