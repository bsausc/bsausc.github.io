Bangladesh Student Association (BSA) at University of South Carolina (USC) is the official student organization for Bangladeshi students at USC comprising of more than 60 student members. BSA is a non-political and non-profit student organzation working at the best interest of the Bangladeshi students at USC. BSA helps to familiarize Bangladesh to the diverse South Carolina community by presenting the culture and heritage of Bangladesh.
